WHO ARE WE?

Our young and dynamic company was founded in 2014. Through our professionalism and positive drive we strive towards market-wide success.

We have earned our name as a trusted market-leading supplier of Uzbekistan due to our expertise of the local market and our excellent long standing partnership with a valuable local partner, who has been representing the Uzbek pharmaceutical market since 1996.
Due to our partnership, we have established a vast network distributing products to local pharmacies, clinics, hospitals, beauty salons and physicians.

OUR SERVICES
We are a wholesale distributor of pharmaceuticals, para-pharmaceuticals and medical devices.
Our products range from cosmetics to medicine, aesthetics, probiotics, supplements and others.
The services we provide are not only aimed at the distribution and marketing of our offered products, but they also include the licencing and regulatory hurdles involved in registering products, as well as proper pharmacovigilance procedures within the Uzbekistan territory.

Our team includes marketing and sales specialists, sales representatives, medical representatives, in-store promoters and merchandisers, supported by graphic designers, regulatory affairs, logistics officers and our business development department.
Our Marketing department consists of sales and medical representatives as well as graphic designers and digital marketing experts, capable of producing all our marketing material and carrying out traditional and digital marketing campaigns.
We value ethics and we ensure ethical behavior before our partners on both ends, not to mention ethical behavior before the patient and end-consumer. Our team ensures the reputation of all parties remains intact.
We value our customers and ensure to go the full mile for them.
OUR AIM
Our aim is to become the leading distributor for health care and allied products in Uzbekistan.
We are fully GDP licensed and it is our aim to adhere to these standards.
